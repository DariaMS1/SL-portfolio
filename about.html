About Sofia Denise

My name is Sofia Denise and I am a freelance illustrator that specialises in comics and character design. Throughout the years I experimented with different styles and themes, the one which stuck is gothic, dark and mysterious, in all its forms. I enjoy narrative based projects which can be a multi volume book series or a series of images the come together as a short story. I hope to create illustrations that allow the reader an escape from reality and get sucked into the worlds I create. In terms of character design, I enjoy playing around with all the possible designs I could make for characters with their world, circumstance and personality in mind.
